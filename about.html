Hi there,

I'm Garima, and this is the story of how PlanO came to be—a journey that started with the life-changing experience of becoming a mom.

I thought I was ready. I devoured every parenting book, followed all the "experts" on social media, and subscribed to every parenting website. I was armed with knowledge, prepared for anything... or so I naively believed.

But motherhood is a crash course in humility. No amount of pre-baby prep truly prepares you for the overwhelming, exhausting, and exhilarating reality of raising a tiny human. Suddenly, I was juggling a demanding career, a whirlwind of a child, and the remnants of a social life—all while fueled by caffeine and sheer willpower.

Then came the dreaded "terrible twos," and with them, a new parental obsession: finding the perfect activities to nurture my son's curiosity and development. What began as a simple search quickly spiraled into a full-time job. Hours melted away scrolling through Google, trying to figure out what a 2-year-old could even learn, then searching for classes nearby, hunting down reviews... it was a nightmare!

In a world where technology has connected us and AI is reshaping industries, the kids' activity space felt stuck in the past. I was relying on word-of-mouth—word-of-mouth!—to find a decent class. Even after enrolling my child, the scheduling, the sign-ups, the frantic manual calendar entries… it was a constant, low-hum of stress.

Somewhere between a maze of browser tabs and sticky notes, it hit me: There had to be a better way.

✨ And that’s how PlanO was born.

PlanO isn’t just an app; it’s a lifeline for parents. It’s about unlocking the best opportunities for our children—making it ridiculously easy to discover, book, and manage all their activities in one beautifully simple place.

But more than that, I want PlanO to be a trusted partner for parents, a tool that brings a little sanity back into our already chaotic lives.

This isn’t just a business—it’s a mission. I’m building PlanO for every parent who has ever felt overwhelmed, for every child who deserves access to enriching experiences, and for a future where technology truly helps us raise our kids.

I'm incredibly excited to share this journey with you. Join me—let’s make parenting just a little easier, together.

- Garima
